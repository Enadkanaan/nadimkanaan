About

Leader in power and energy systems

Nadim Kanan, PhD is VP of Energy Storage at BrightNight Power with 15+ years of experience advancing renewable energy and energy storage. He brings a proven track record in driving large-scale innovation, guiding cross-functional teams, and delivering strategies that optimize performance, reduce costs, and accelerate the clean energy transition.

He leads initiatives in battery systems engineering, procurement strategy, and portfolio optimization, while also serving as a subject matter expert in renewable energy M&A. His work has enabled organizations to evaluate emerging technologies, execute high-value partnerships, and deliver sustainable solutions with measurable ROI.

A recognized innovator with 17 patents, Nadim is passionate about shaping the future of energy by aligning technical excellence with business strategy to unlock growth and long-term value.
